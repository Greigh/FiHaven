Getting started

What is FiHaven, exactly?

A calm workspace for recurring bills, credit cards, loans, spending, and payoff planning. You enter what you owe and when it’s due; FiHaven shows what’s left this month, what’s coming up, and how extra payments would move your debt.

It’s live on the web today. Native iOS and Android apps are in development and aren’t on the App Store or Google Play yet.

Do I need to link my bank?

No. FiHaven is designed to work fully by hand. Bank linking through Plaid is an optional Pro feature if you want balances and transactions imported alongside what you typed in.

Imported transactions never overwrite your manual entries — they sit next to them.

Is FiHaven a bank or financial advisor?

No. FiHaven doesn’t hold your money, move funds, or give personalized financial advice. It’s software you use to organize information you already have.

Free vs Pro

What’s included for free?

Unlimited bills, cards, and loans; dashboard; budget and rolling pay periods; manual spending log; savings goals; net worth; income sources; mark-paid / partial payments / skip-this-month; JSON and CSV export; and full account deletion.

See the full comparison on Pricing.

What does FiHaven Pro add?
  • Payoff planner (snowball, avalanche, minimums)
  • Calendar view and private iCal feed
  • Full payment history
  • Rewards optimizer for credit cards
  • Subscription finder (recurring charges and price hikes)
  • Per-category budgets
  • Optional Plaid bank linking
  • Autopay auto-mark on due dates
How do I upgrade or cancel Pro?

On the web, open FiHaven Pro from the account menu after you sign in. Checkout runs through Stripe; you can manage or cancel from the billing portal there. When the native apps ship, in-app purchases will go through Apple or Google under their subscription settings.

Promo codes, when offered, can be redeemed in the same Pro dialog.

How features behave

How do bill schedules work?

Bills aren’t limited to monthly. You can set weekly, bi-weekly, quarterly, or annual schedules, plus optional start and end dates for things that don’t run forever. Due dates flow through the dashboard, upcoming list, budget obligations, calendar (Pro), and autopay (Pro).

What does autopay auto-mark do?

If you turn it on for a bill or card (Pro), FiHaven marks the item paid on its due date when you’ve indicated it’s on autopay. It’s a convenience layer — you can still edit or undo payment history.

What is the subscription finder?

A Pro tool that surfaces recurring charges you might have forgotten — from bills you flagged as subscriptions and from spending patterns (same merchant across multiple months). It also calls out price increases when it spots them.

What is the rewards optimizer?

FiHaven ships a built-in database of common card reward categories. Pro suggests which card to use for a purchase category based on the cards you’ve added. Adding or editing a card can auto-suggest a preset from that database.

Can I track 0% promo APR deadlines?

Yes. Enter promo balance, rate, and end date on a card. The dashboard highlights cards whose 0% window is ending and the payoff planner (Pro) can factor promo minimums into strategies.

Your data & account

Do you sell my data or show ads?

No ads, no data brokerage. FiHaven makes money from optional Pro subscriptions — not from selling your financial picture. See Privacy for the full picture.

Can I export or delete everything?

Yes. Export JSON or CSV from Settings anytime. Account deletion removes your user record and associated data from our servers. Disconnecting Plaid deletes the access token and linked account data.

What sign-in security is available?

Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. Sessions use secure HttpOnly cookies on the web. You can add an authenticator app (TOTP), email codes, or a passkey. Sign-up and sign-in are rate-limited and protected by Cloudflare Turnstile. More detail on Security.

Is FiHaven open source?

The code is on GitHub for transparency, but FiHaven is source available, not open source — you can read and contribute, but you may not run a public hosted copy or redistribute modified builds without permission. See the license.

Mobile apps

Are the iPhone and Android apps available yet?

Not on the public stores yet. We’re building native apps that will use the same account and API as the website. Until they launch, use FiHaven in your browser — it’s the full product today.
