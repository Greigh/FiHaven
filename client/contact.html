Contact

Get the right human (it’s usually just me)

FiHaven is built and operated by Daniel Hipskind — not a support farm. Sending your note to the right place means a faster, useful reply.

Try in the app first — many answers live in FAQ or Settings: export your data (JSON/CSV), disconnect Plaid, manage Pro billing (Stripe portal), change password/MFA, or delete your account entirely. No email required for any of that.

Where to send what

Product & billing help

Confused by a feature, stuck on onboarding, promo code didn’t work, or Stripe charged unexpectedly.

support@fihaven.app

Privacy & your data

Privacy questions, export/deletion help, or exercising access rights under GDPR/CCPA.

security@fihaven.app

Security issues

Suspected vulnerabilities or account compromise. Please don’t post these publicly.

Private GitHub advisory
security@fihaven.app

Bugs & feature ideas

Reproducible bugs or thoughtful feature requests — especially if you can point at the screen or attach steps.

GitHub Issues

What to include

  • The email on your FiHaven account (never your password).
  • Browser or device, and whether you’re on web or testing a native build.
  • For billing: whether you subscribed on the web (Stripe) or, later, through Apple/Google.
  • For bugs: what you expected, what happened, and the smallest steps to reproduce.

Response time

There’s no 24/7 desk — this is a small product with real users, not enterprise SLA territory. Security reports get priority. Most other mail gets a reply within a few business days. If your account is locked out, say so in the subject line.

Mail you’ll get from us

Transactional email only: verification, password reset, security codes, due-date reminders if you opt in, and occasional account notices. From no-reply@fihaven.app; replies to support mail route to support@fihaven.app. We don’t send marketing newsletters.
