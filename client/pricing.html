How Pro billing works

  • Web today: subscribe through Stripe after you sign in (account menu → FiHaven Pro). Monthly, 3-month, and yearly intervals may be offered — exact price is always shown at checkout before you pay.
  • Cancel anytime: use the Stripe billing portal from the same Pro dialog. Access continues through the period you’ve paid for.
  • App stores later: when the iOS and Android apps launch, in-app Pro will bill through Apple or Google. Your entitlement is computed on our server, so Pro follows your account across platforms.
  • Promo codes: occasionally offered; redeem in the Pro dialog. Terms are stated with each code.
  • Plaid is optional: FiHaven works fully without bank linking. Pro automation layers on top without overwriting manual entries.
