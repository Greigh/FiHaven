Bills on any schedule

Monthly, weekly, bi-weekly, quarterly, or annual — with optional start and end dates. Separate autopay day from due date. See what's due, overdue, or already paid at a glance.

Cards & 0% promos

Track balances, utilization, and exactly how much per month clears a 0% promo before it expires — with card presets from a rewards database.

Loans & mortgages

Keep loans in their own tab with a recommended payment that matches your minimum — not the whole principal — and pay-in-full when you want.

Budget & runway

Budget lenses (50/30/20, safe-to-spend, debt-focus, and more), income sources, savings goals, net worth, and a cushion-after-bills view — no spreadsheet required.

Spending log

Record manual purchases by category alongside your bills. With Pro bank linking, imports layer on top — flag duplicates and keep what you entered by hand.

Payoff planner Pro

Compare snowball, avalanche, and minimums-only side-by-side. See your debt-free date and what each strategy saves in interest.

Rewards, perks & offers Pro

See which card earns the most per category — plus track recurring perks, activated Amex/Chase offers, and merchant lookup while a 0% promo is active.

Subscription finder Pro

Surfaces recurring charges, price hikes, and subscriptions you might have forgotten — from flagged bills and merchant patterns.

History & calendar Pro

Searchable payment history with edit and export, plus a due-date calendar and private iCal feed for your other apps.

Family sharing

Share bills, cards, and goals with a partner or household. Joining is free with an invite; the Family plan unlocks creating and managing a household of up to three people.

Own your data

Download everything as JSON or CSV anytime. Delete your account and every byte is gone. No lock-in, no ads.

How it works

Three steps to a clear month.

  1. 01

    Add your bills, cards & loans

    Drop in recurring payments, card balances, loan minimums, and any 0% promo terms. Pick weekly, monthly, or other schedules — the dashboard fills in instantly.

  2. 02

    Mark things paid as you go

    One click on a bill or card moves it to "Paid" with the date, amount, and a note if you want.

  3. 03

    Plan ahead with confidence

    See what's left in the month, what an extra $200 toward debt would buy you, and what comes next month.

Your account is protected

Boring security, on purpose.

Protected sign-in

A quick Cloudflare Turnstile check keeps automated bots out of the login form. Real users barely notice.

Encrypted passwords

Your password is hashed with bcrypt on the server and is never stored in plain text. Even we can't read it.

Secure sessions

You stay signed in through a private HttpOnly cookie only your browser can use. Changing your password signs out every other device.

Passkeys & two-factor

Passwordless passkey sign-in on web, iPhone, and Android — plus authenticator app, email codes, and Face ID app lock on iOS.

Everywhere you are

Web, iPhone, iPad — and Android.

One account syncs across the web app and native clients. Add a bill on your phone, mark it paid on your laptop — same data, same calm dashboard. Bank linking and Pro features work on fihaven.app today.
