How it works

Three steps to a clear month.

  1. 01

    Add your bills and cards

    Drop in your recurring payments, balances, and any 0% promo terms. The dashboard fills in instantly.

  2. 02

    Mark things paid as you go

    One click on a bill or card moves it to "Paid" with the date, amount, and a note if you want.

  3. 03

    Plan ahead with confidence

    See what's left in the month, what an extra $200 toward debt would buy you, and what comes next month.

Your account is protected

Boring security, on purpose.

Protected sign-in

A quick Cloudflare Turnstile check keeps automated bots out of the login form. Real users barely notice.

Encrypted passwords

Your password is hashed with bcrypt on the server and is never stored in plain text. Even we can't read it.

Secure sessions

You stay signed in through a private HttpOnly cookie only your browser can use. Changing your password signs out every other device.
