Display name

Shown in the navbar's "Signed in as" panel. Leave blank to fall back to your email.

Time zone

Controls what "today" means when FiHaven counts down to a due date. Defaults to your browser's detected zone — change this if you travel often or want a fixed reference.

Currency

How amounts are shown across FiHaven — sets the symbol and number formatting everywhere.

Default view

Which tab opens first when you go to your dashboard.

Budget period

Choose how a period is defined for paid/owed tracking. Calendar months are the default. A custom start day (e.g. the 25th) groups early-next-month bills into the period you'd plan for. A rolling window tracks a fixed number of days.

Budget rule

Optional budget lens on the Budget tab — split presets, safe-to-spend, debt focus, or envelope lite (Pro). Housing and debt ratio warnings appear when relevant. Off by default.

Planned dollars above minimums — same idea as the Payoff planner extra payment.

Payments

Choose how much you need to pay before a bill or card counts as fully paid. Anything less shows as a partial payment with the remainder still due. Bills always use their full amount; this mainly affects credit cards.

Dashboard

Control what appears on your home dashboard.

    Calendar subscription

    Subscribe to your bill & card due dates from any calendar app (Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook). The URL contains a private token — rotate it if you ever share or accidentally publish the link.

    No subscription URL yet. Generate one to start syncing.

    Google Calendar: Other calendars → From URL, paste the URL.
